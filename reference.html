The 8 steps of your pitch

①Greet
Zǎoshang hǎo. Lǎoshī hǎo. Dàjiā hǎo. Wǒ jiào ___.
Good morning. Hello teacher. Hello everyone. My name is ___.

Repeat steps ② – ⑦ TWICE — once for each family member.

②Identify
Zhè shì wǒ de ___.
This is my ___. (bàba / māma / gēge / gǒu / māo …)
③Name
Wǒ de ___ jiào ___.
My ___ is called ___.
④Age ⚠ no shì!
Wǒ de ___ ___ suì.
My ___ is ___ years old. (Never: Wǒ de bàba shì 45 suì)
⑤Nationality ⚠ rén required!
Tā shì ___ rén.
He / She is ___ (Chinese / Australian / American …). Never: Tā shì Àodàlìyà.
⑥Description
Wǒ de ___ hěn ___, [hé / dànshì] hěn ___.
1 appearance + 1 personality. Link with hé (and) or dànshì (but). Always say hěn before the adjective!
⑦Location
Tā zhù zài ___.
He / She lives in ___ (Xīní / Běijīng / Shànghǎi …).
⑧Sign off
Xièxie. Zàijiàn!
Thank you. Goodbye!

The 6 deadly errors — memorise!

  1. No shì with age — say ___ suì, never shì ___ suì.
  2. No shì before adjectives — use hěn instead.
  3. Always end nationality with rén (人).
  4. Word order: hěn FIRST, then adjective. Never gāo hěn.
  5. Location needs zhù zài — never just zhù.
  6. No de between hěn and the adjective. Just hěn + adj.
